2' x 3' CNC Laser

The CNC Laser 2x3 machine is designed for space-conscious users who need precision and versatility. Its compact size makes it ideal for hobbyists, small businesses, and educational institutions working with materials such as acrylic, wood, leather, and paper. Despite its smaller footprint, it delivers sharp detailing, clean edges, and consistent results for small-format cutting and engraving applications.

Technical Specifications

  • Model: 6090
  • Cutting Area : 600MM X 900MM (2 * 3)
  • Laser Type : CO2 Glass Tube Laser
  • Laser Power : 80W Max. (Can Cut Acrylic up to 15 mm)
  • Cooling : Integrated Compressor based Industrial Chiller with water flow pump. Can keep the laser tube temp. at 16 degree Celsius or below than that
  • Red Dot pointer to know exact position of Origin
  • Direct Cutting From Corel Draw or any Vector editing Software
  • Direct USB interface or can import files from USB flash drive
  • Air Pump, Exhaust Pump, Water Pump, Water Chiller, Exhaust Pipe, All included.
  • Application Area : Sign Making, Trophy Making, Name Plate, Number Plates, wood Engrave, Acrylic Cutting, Cloth Engrave, Mica Engrave, Glass Engrave, Paper cutting.
  • Controller Trozon
