Konica 512/42PL Flex Printing Machine

The Konica 4 Head Flex Printing Machine 512 (42 PL) is an advanced automatic flex printer designed for high-quality solvent printing with Japanese and German precision components. It is equipped with Konica Minolta printheads, a Panasonic servo motor, and an Ultraprint RIP system, ensuring exceptional print quality and high-speed performance.
