4' x 4' CNC Laser

The CNC Laser 4x4 is a robust solution for businesses needing a larger work surface for frequent or large-scale jobs. It can efficiently process full 4x4 ft sheets of MDF, acrylic, or other compatible materials, allowing for faster throughput and fewer material cuts. Its size and performance make it ideal for interior decor, packaging prototypes, and medium-volume production.
