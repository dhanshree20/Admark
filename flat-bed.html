UV Digital Flatbed Printer

The UV Digital Flatbed Printer is a high-performance printing solution designed for direct printing on multiple materials like wood, ceramic, glass, metal, acrylic, and more. Equipped with UV inkjet technology, it provides sharp, vibrant prints with excellent adhesion and durability.

Technical Specifications

  • Usage/Application: Printing
  • Printing Technology: UV Inkjet
  • Ink Type: UV Curable Ink
  • Printing Resolution: 720 x 1440 dpi
  • Printing Size: 2.5 x 1.25 m
  • Bed Size: 2 x 3 m
  • Country of Origin: Made in India
  • Automatic Material Thickness Measurement for precise and efficient printing
  • Anti-Collision Device - Carriage automatically stops when colliding with obstacles
  • Negative Pressure System - Ensures smooth ink flow and prevents ink leakage
  • Prints on Various Materials including Wood, Ceramic, Metal, PVC, Acrylic, Glass, and More
  • High Ink Adhesion - Even though it prints on almost any substrate, adhesion levels vary
